# Past Indicative

## English Equivalents

### Completed Actions

Describing completed actions

| Spanish               | English                  |
| --------------------- | ------------------------ |
| Fui a la tienda.      | I went to the store.     |
| Terminé mi tarea.     | I finished my homework.  |
| Le dije la verdad.    | I told her the truth.    |

## Spanish Differences

### Ser / Estar

Describing uninterrupted events

| Spanish                                                  | English                      |
| -------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| La fiesta fue divertida.                                 | The party was fun.           |
| La fiesta era divertida hasta que llegó la policía. ⇐ Interrupted |                    |
| El libro fue interesante.                                | The book was interesting.    |
| El libro era interesante hasta que leí el capítulo cinco. ⇐ Interrupted |              |

Describing the characteristics of people and things during a specific time

| Spanish                                   | English                  |
| ----------------------------------------- | ------------------------ |
| Juan fue presidente de 2009 a 2017.       | Juan was president.      |
| Juan era presidente. ⇐ No specific timeframe |                       |
| Maria estuvo en Madrid por dos años.      | Maria was in Madrid.     |
| Maria estaba en Madrid. ⇐ No specific timeframe |                    |
| El café estuvo caliente por cinco minutos. | The coffee was hot.     |
| El café estaba caliente. ⇐ No specific timeframe |                   |

### Saber

"Saber" changes meanings in the imperfect and indicative

| Spanish                                      | English                                    |
| -------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| Supe que ella estaba diciendo la verdad.     | I found out she was telling the truth.     |
| Sabía que no era una buena idea.             | I knew it wasn't a good idea.              |

### Conocer

"Conocer" changes meanings in the imperfect and indicative

| Spanish                            | English                             |
| ---------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Conocí a Juan en 2010.             | I met Juan in 2010.                 |
| María me dijo que conocía a Juan.  | Maria told me that she knew Juan.   |

### Poder

"Poder" changes meanings in the imperfect and indicative

| Spanish                   | English                              |
| ------------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| Pude levantar la caja.    | I could (and I did) lift the box.    |
| Podía levantar la caja.   | I could (hypothetically) lift the box. |

### Querer

"Querer" changes meanings in the imperfect and indicative

| Spanish                   | English                                  |
| ------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Quise abrir la puerta.    | I wanted (and I tried) to open the door. |
| No quise trabajar.        | I refused to work.                       |
| Quería ir al cine.        | I wanted to go to the movies.            |

### Tener

"Tener" changes meanings in the imperfect and indicative

| Spanish                      | English                               |
| ---------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| Tuve que irme de la fiesta.  | I had to (and I did) leave the party. |
| Tenía que ir a la tienda.    | I had to (pending action) go to the store. |
| Tuve dolor de cabeza.        | I got (at that moment) a headache.    |
| Tenía dolor de cabeza.       | I had a headache.                     |

### Haber

Use "hubo" for completed events

| Spanish                                | English                            |
| -------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Hubo un accidente en la autopista. ⇐ Completed event | There was an accident on the freeway. |
| Hubo un concierto en el parque. ⇐ Completed event | There was a concert in the park. |
| Había mucho tráfico.                   | There was a lot of traffic.        |
| Había naranjas en el mercado.          | There were oranges at the market.  |

### Deber

Use "deber" in the past indicative for "supposed to" for obligations

| Spanish                  | English                             |
| ------------------------ | ----------------------------------- |
| Debí ir a la tienda.     | I was supposed to go to the store.  |
