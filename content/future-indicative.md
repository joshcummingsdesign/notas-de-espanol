# Future Indicative

## English Equivalents

### Going to Do

Instead of the traditional future tense conjugation, use "going to" to describe actions in the future (ir + a + infinitive)

| English                                   | Spanish                          |
| ----------------------------------------- | -------------------------------- |
| I'm going to eat something.               | Voy a comer algo.                |
| I'm going to travel to Mexico in July.    | Voy a viajar a México en julio.  |
| I'm going to go to the grocery store on Monday. | Voy a ir al súper el lunes. |
