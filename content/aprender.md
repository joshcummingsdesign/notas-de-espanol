# Aprender

**Aprender** means "to learn" in Spanish. It's a regular -er verb.

## Present Tense

| Person | Conjugation |
|--------|-------------|
| yo | aprendo |
| tú | aprendes |
| él/ella/usted | aprende |
| nosotros | aprendemos |
| vosotros | aprendéis |
| ellos/ellas/ustedes | aprenden |

## Example Sentences

- **Yo aprendo español** - I learn Spanish
- **Ella aprende rápido** - She learns quickly
- **Nosotros aprendemos juntos** - We learn together

## Common Phrases

- **aprender de memoria** - to learn by heart
- **aprender a + infinitive** - to learn to (do something)
  - *Quiero aprender a bailar* - I want to learn to dance

## Related Words

- **el aprendizaje** - learning, apprenticeship
- **el/la aprendiz** - apprentice, learner
- **aprendido** - learned (past participle)
