# Present Indicative

The present indicative is used to describe who, what, or where something is. It’s also used to ask simple questions, describe habitual actions, check for permission, or to express near-future events.

## English Equivalents

### Descriptions

Describing who, what, or where something is

| English | Spanish |
|---------|---------|
| I’m American. | Soy estadounidense. |
| He lives in Mexico. | Vive en México. |
| He likes to read. | Le gusta leer. |
| I’m in the kitchen. | Estoy en la cocina. |
| The garden is in front of the house. | El jardín está delante de la casa. |
| They are across from the school. | Están enfrente de la escuela. |

### Questions

Asking simple questions

| English | Spanish |
|---------|---------|
| Do you want to go to the movies tomorrow? | ¿Quieres ir al cine mañana? |
| Can you help me next week? | ¿Me puedes ayudar la próxima semana? |
| How goes the job? | ¿Cómo va el trabajo? |

### Habitual Actions

Describing something that generally happens

| English | Spanish |
|---------|---------|
| I lie down at 9:30pm. | Me acuesto a las nueve y media de la noche. |
| He walks on Tuesdays. | Camina los martes. |
| Every day I drink coffee. | Todos los días tomo café. |

## Spanish Differences

### Near-Future Events

Expressing near-future events

| English | Spanish |
|---------|---------|
| I'll call you. ⇐ I call | Te llamo. |
| I'll be right back. ⇐ I return | Ahorita vuelvo. |
| I'll let you know. ⇐ I advise | Te aviso. |
| If the car is a good price, I'll buy it. ⇐ I buy | Si el carro está a buen precio, lo compro. |
| If the weather is nice tomorrow, we'll go to the beach. ⇐ We go | Si mañana hace buen tiempo, vamos a la playa. |

### If-Then Statements

Present tense If-then statements are in the indicative

| English | Spanish |
|---------|---------|
| If I go to the store, I'll buy apples. | Si voy a la tienda, compro manzanas. |

### Permission

Checking for permission

| English | Spanish |
|---------|---------|
| Should I send it to you? ⇐ I send it to you? | ¿Te lo envío? |
| Should we leave? ⇐ We leave? | ¿Nos vamos? |
| Does that seem good to you? ⇐ It seems good to you? | ¿Te parece bien? |
