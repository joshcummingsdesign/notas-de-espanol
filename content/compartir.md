# Compartir

"Compartir" means "to share." It's a regular -ir verb.

## Present Indicative

| Pronoun             | Conjugation  |
| ------------------- | ------------ |
| yo                  | comparto     |
| tú                  | compartes    |
| él/ella/usted       | comparte     |
| nosotros            | compartimos  |
| ellos/ellas/ustedes | comparten    |

## Common Uses

| Spanish                          | English                             |
| -------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Comparto mi comida contigo.      | I share my food with you.           |
| ¿Compartes tu habitación?        | Do you share your room?             |
| Ella comparte sus ideas.         | She shares her ideas.               |
| Compartimos los gastos.          | We share the expenses.              |
| Ellos comparten un apartamento.  | They share an apartment.            |
